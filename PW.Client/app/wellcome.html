Wellcome to the "Parrot Wings" application!

This application is designed for Parrot Wings (PW, virtual money) transfer between system users.

Transactions data is refreshed in real time through the use of SignalR technology.

Use  Sign In button on the navigation bar for Login or Registration.

On successful registration every User will be awarded with 500 (five hundred) PW starting balance.

Users login to the system using their email and password.
